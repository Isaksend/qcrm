To determine the positioning of TinyCRM, it is necessary to examine the competitive landscape of global and regional providers.

\section{Global Enterprise Platforms}
Salesforce holds approximately 21 percent of the global market share and is renowned for its depth and customization capabilities \cite{salesforce2025market}. However, for a micro-business, it presents a steep learning curve and a high total cost of ownership, often requiring certified partners and one to six months for implementation \cite{hubspot2026comparison}. HubSpot offers a more intuitive interface, but its pricing can become expensive as a business scales its data requirements, with some small businesses paying up to 800 USD per month for basic reporting and automation features \cite{hubspot2026comparison}.
\section{Regional and Localized Systems}
Bitrix24 and amoCRM are the dominant regional players. Bitrix24 is an all-in-one platform bundling customer management with task management and videoconferencing \cite{hubspot2026comparison}. While cost-effective, its interface is frequently described as crowded and overwhelming for teams that only require basic sales tracking \cite{hubspot2026comparison}. amoCRM focuses on messenger-based sales, which aligns with communication habits in Kazakhstan, but it lacks the advanced predictive intelligence layer required by users who seek data-driven prompts.
